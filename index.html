Parent Guide

1. The Goal: A low-stimulation environment to help wind down. No scores, no levels, just flow.

2. Hidden Menu: Long-press the "Lullaby Loop" title at the top for 3 seconds to access Settings (Timer, Sound, Pause).

3. Sunset Timer: The screen will slowly dim and the audio will fade out automatically over the selected duration.

4. Tips: Use "Guided Access" (iOS) or "App Pinning" (Android) to keep your child inside the app.
